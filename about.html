Hi, I am Yara!

Thank you for clicking this far! I am business analyst living in Brisbane, currently dabbling in the programming space as part of the SheCodes Plus 2023 cohort. I hold a Dual Bachelors Degree in Information Technology and Accounting and have worked as an analyst for a software development team within the last five years. In this role, I am responsible for requirement gathering and analysis, testing and supporting our development team to ensure we provide the correct digital solution to our customers. I enjoy going for walks and hikes, albeit the weather disagreeing with me at times. I also like to cook (and eating out), diy projects and dabbling into photo editing and making reels.
